Welcome to Koda Kafe!

Located at the University of Houston Sugarland campus

What We Offer Students



Koda Kafe is more than just a dining hall; we're a hub for students to unwind, connect, and recharge in the heart of UH Sugarland. With cozy seating areas and a welcoming ambiance, we provide the perfect backdrop for studying, socializing, or simply taking a moment for yourself.

Convenience is key for busy students, which is why we offer flexible hours that align with your schedule and a location that's just steps away from your next class. Plus, our commitment to affordability means you can enjoy our offerings without breaking the bank. Whether you need a quick caffeine fix between classes or a hearty meal to power through a study session, Koda Kafe has you covered.

In addition, we offer students from the main campus a way of utilizing their already-owned meal plans! Just swipe your card at the front and use a meal exchange for several food options. We accept cougar cash as well.

Join us at Koda Kafe and discover why we're more than just a café – we're a home away from home for the entire UH Sugarland student community.
